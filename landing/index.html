How it works

From a QR code to a daily coach in two minutes.

No download, no dashboard, no learning curve. The whole thing is a conversation.

1

Scan & say hi

Point your camera at the code or tap the link. No download, no password. Baseline greets you in WhatsApp.

2

Answer a few questions

Your goal, your age, a little about you. A short, friendly chat, never a form to fill out.

3

Connect your device in one tap

A single Google sign in gives read only access to your steps, sleep, and heart rate. Nothing technical.

4

Get coached daily

One grounded tip a day, learned from your own baseline. Log meals and workouts whenever you like.

What it does

Everything happens in the chat you already use.

📸

Snap your meals

Send a photo and Baseline estimates calories, protein, carbs, and fat, plus what's left for the day.

🏃

Track workouts

Just say 'ran 30 min' and Baseline logs it in seconds, keeping your streak alive. No forms.

💬

Ask 'why?'

Every tip can be questioned. Baseline explains its reasoning, grounded in your own numbers.

🔒

Calm by design

One decision a day. Never a feed, never a nag, never a scary red number, never a diagnosis.

🔒
Privacy

Your data stays yours.

Baseline reads only your steps, sleep, and heart rate. Never your messages, your photos beyond the meals you send, your location, or your contacts. Your raw health numbers live in a private screen, never dumped into the chat. Disconnect anytime by replying 'stop.'
